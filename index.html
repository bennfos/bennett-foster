Projects

winnow

  • Oct 2019

Marcus Aurelius wrote that we should winnow our thoughts, so that we always have something meaningful to think and talk about. Winnow is a lifestyle app that allows users to filter out the chaff of media noise by creating a daily journal of quotes from their favorite authors, speakers and thinkers. Winnow provides inspirational quotes for each day of the year, which users can meditate upon and add their own thoughts and reflections. Alternatively, users can take on the project of making a quote book from scratch, which will help them hone their own personal philosophy of life.
